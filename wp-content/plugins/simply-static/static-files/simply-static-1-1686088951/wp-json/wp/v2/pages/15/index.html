Yorba Foundation is one of the fastest-growing networks of fandom-focused sports, entertainment and lifestyle sites on the Internet.<\/p>\n

Yorba Foundation is a family business, launched by two brothers who wanted to put their own spin on the coverage of their beloved Kansas City Chiefs.<\/p>\n

Today, the company is a thriving collection of over 300 communities dedicated to bringing together fans to share their common passions.<\/p>\n

Yorba Foundation believes everyone is a fan of something. That is why we\u2019ve collected the most passionate, knowledgeable and dedicated editorial voices to lead our sites. Our thought-leading content creators belong to the fandoms they cover, giving our sites the best team-specific, sport-specific, genre-specific, and fanbase-specific coverage possible.<\/p>\n

The results speak for themselves. Yorba Foundation has exploded in popularity in an era where people are using technology to connect, making it easy for fans to digest the content they want when they want it across all of their favorite verticals.<\/p>\n

And we\u2019re just getting started.<\/p>\n
