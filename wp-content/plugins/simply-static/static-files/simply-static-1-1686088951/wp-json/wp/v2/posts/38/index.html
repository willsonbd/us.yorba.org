What Did Jill Duggar Do After Leaving Her Family? <\/p>\n

Jill and Derick starred in six seasons of Counting On. However, she walked away from the reality TV series in 2017, ahead of its season 7 premiere in 2018. In 2022, Jill and Derick moved into a new home with their growing family, on the border of Oklahoma and Arkansas. \u201cWe moved!! It\u2019s been a crazy several weeks for us, but we are finally settling into our new place!\u201d Jill shared in her family blog. DillardFamily<\/a>. She also revealed that Derick had a new job, He had been sworn in as an attorney in Little Rock, Arkansas, and then started the transfer process for his new public service job as a prosecutor in Oklahoma.<\/p>\n

Jill and Derick welcomed their third son, another boy, in July 2022. Jill appears to be a stay-at-home, while Derick is busy with his courtroom job. Jill announced that she would be releasing her Tell-All memoir in 2024. Called Counting the Cost, it outlines the changes they faced, \u201cincluding lack of respect for boundaries, greed, manipulation, and betrayal,\u201d as per Jill<\/a>\u2019s Instagram post. Jill then participated in Shiny Happy People: Duggar Family Secrets, which premiered on June 2, 2023.<\/p>\n

Why Did Jill Duggar Leave? <\/p>\n

Interestingly, Jill is the only Duggar kid who appears in the docuseries. According to NickiSwift<\/a>, Jill and her Dillard family stopped filming for Counting On due to backlash. This happened when Derick’s controversial tweets about transgender co-star Jazz Jennings surfaced online.<\/p>\n

In October 2020, Jill and Derick took to their YouTube DillardFamilyOfficial<\/a> to reveal that they left because they wanted to focus on pursuing their other passions. They added that they wished to, “pursue our own goals and everything.” They also noted that it was a, “good decision” for them, even if leaving their lives as reality TV stars behind was, “difficult.” They didn’t shed light on exactly what these goals were at the time.<\/p>\n

Jill and Derick weren\u2019t on the, \u201cbest terms\u201d with some of her family after having disagreements, but looked forward to having some time to heal. “There’s been some distancing there,” Jill had said about her relationship with her family. However, she did not name any members in particular. In Shiny Happy People: Duggar Family Secrets, Jill looks back at her family\u2019s fall from grace, and Josh’s scandal, while opening up about her life and upbringing as a Duggar.<\/p>\n
