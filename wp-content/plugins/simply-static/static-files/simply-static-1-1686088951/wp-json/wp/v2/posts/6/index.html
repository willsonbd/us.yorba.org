The PGA Tour will merge with LIV Golf, ending a years-long rivalry between the two world golfing superpowers.\u00a0<\/em><\/strong><\/p>\n

After years of controversy, the PGA Tour and LIV Golf are expected to engage in a company merger to create a \u201cglobal entity\u201d of golfing.<\/p>\n

The inaugural season for LIV Golf League, which is financially backed by the Saudi Arabia Public Investment Fund,\u00a0officially started in October 2022. Several high-profile golfers joined the circuit, including recent PGA Tour winner Brooks Kopeka and golfing legend Phil Mickelson.<\/p>\n

LIV\u2019s emergence caused quite a stir in the golfing world, with the PGA essentially shunning golfers who joined the new league and engaging in a long legal battle that will now come to a peaceful conclusion.<\/p>\n

LIV Golf and the PGA Tour will merge, ending feud<\/p>\n

\u201cAfter two years of disruption and distraction, this is a historic day for the game we all know and love,\u201d\u00a0said PGA Tour commissioner Jay Monahan.<\/p>\n

Previously, the two sides were engaged in bitter litigation over contract disputes, which will now be put to rest in favor of unity and financial gain. The two sides will form a \u201cnew, yet-to-be-named for-profit company\u201d that will also include the DP World Tour (or, European PGA Tour).<\/p>\n

Per CNBC\u2019s David Faber, the Saudi P.I.F. is \u201cprepared to invest billions [of dollars] of new capital into the new entity.\u201d The merger will also create a \u201ca fair and objective process\u201d for previously renounced players to re-apply for PGA membership,\u00a0according to the official press release.<\/p>\n

The conflict between the PGA and LIV\u00a0has been palpable. This resolution will reunite great golfers on the course while presumably leading to more investment in the game of golf and its presentation around the world. Many will say it was inevitable given the volume of noteworthy names who left the PGA Tour. In a way, this is a merger of tradition and innovation, the old and the new.
